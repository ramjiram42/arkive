# 📙 Team Quick Start Guide

Welcome to **ARKIVE**, our new document and asset management portal!
Here's how to get the most out of it:

### Finding Files Fast
Use the top **Search Bar** to instantly find documents. You can also filter by categories (Docs, Images, Video).

### Changing Your View
- Click the Grid/List toggle in the top right to switch between thumbnail and detailed views.
- Click on table headers (Name, Modified, Size) to sort files ascending or descending.

### Organizing
- Click the **Star** icon next to any critical file to add it to your "Starred" view.
- Use the sidebar to quickly access your Recent or Shared files.

### Dark Mode
Working late? Click the Moon/Sun icon in the top right header to switch themes beautifully.
