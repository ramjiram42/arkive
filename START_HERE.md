# 🚀 ARKIVE Deployment Roadmap

Welcome to your ARKIVE project! This guide will get your application live and ready for your team.

## Getting Started

1. **Review checklist**: Open `DEPLOYMENT_CHECKLIST.md` to track your progress.
2. **Push to GitHub**: Follow instructions in `DEPLOYMENT_GUIDE.md` to get your code onto GitHub.
3. **Deploy to Vercel**: Connect your GitHub repo to Vercel for instant deployment (see `DEPLOYMENT_GUIDE.md`).
4. **Share**: Once live, send the `TEAM_QUICK_START.md` guide to your colleagues along with your new URL!

*Everything has been pre-configured for a seamless deployment experience.*
